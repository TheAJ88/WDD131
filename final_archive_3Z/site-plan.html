Overview

Purpose

To showcase my writing from a Science Fiction Literature class. I've been meaning to create a portfolio-style website for my writing and have it be more interactive than normal sites. I have learned stuff from my cloud technologies class that would allow me to do so much more but I figured I could get to work on the base with this assignment.

Audience

Casual readers and hardcore science fiction fans. These stories are flashes, ergo, they can be called the tiktoks of writing in the sense that they are shorter than your average short story but big enough to pack provocative and entertaining material.

Dynamic elements

I will be using Javascript to add the following elements : Interactive Story Previews, Search Functionality, Theme Toggle (Light/Dark Mode). Should be pretty easy.
